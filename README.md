# BMI Calculator

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a personâ€™s weight in kilograms  divided by the square of height in meters. A high BMI can indicate high body fatness. BMI screens for weight categories that may lead to health problems, but it does not diagnose the body fatness or health of an individual.


# Working

*Enter your weight (kilograms).<br>
*Enter your height (metres).<br>
*Press Submit button to show the BMI value,BMI category and BMI table.

# How BMI calculated?

BMI is calculated by dividing a person's weight in kilograms by the square of his/her height in meters. <br>

eg: weight=50(kg), height=2.0(m)<br>
Equation = weight / height * height
